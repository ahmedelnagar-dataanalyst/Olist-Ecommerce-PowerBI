# 🛒 Brazilian E-Commerce Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the **Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset** from Kaggle, which contains over **100,000 orders** placed between 2017 and 2018. The goal is to extract actionable business insights across sales performance, delivery operations, and customer satisfaction.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions
- What are the top-performing product categories and cities by revenue?
- What is the average delivery time, and how many orders are delivered late?
- Is there a relationship between delivery speed and customer satisfaction?
- Which states have the highest late delivery rates?

---

### 2️⃣ Delivery & Customer Satisfaction

- Late Delivery Rate: **8.11%**
- Average Review Score: **4.10 / 5**
- Sales distribution by city (Map)
- Delivery variance by state
- Average review score by product category

### 3️⃣ Delivery vs Customer Satisfaction by State
- Scatter chart showing the relationship between delivery speed and customer review scores
- Top 10 states by late delivery rate
- State-level breakdown table
- Interactive tooltip showing per-state KPIs on hover

---

## 💡 Key Insight
> States with faster delivery consistently show **higher customer review scores**.  
> States like **SP** (7.47 avg days / 4.20 score) outperform states like **MA** (21.51 avg days / 3.78 score).  
> **Faster delivery = Higher customer satisfaction.**
